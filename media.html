MEDIA

AUDIO

The <audio> tag is used to embed sound content in a document, such as music or other audio streams.

EXAMPLE

            <audio controls>
                <source src="assets/horse.mp3"/>
            </audio>

Click the play button to play de sound.



VIDEO

The <video> tag is used to embed video content in a document, such as a movie clip or other video streams.

EXAMPLE

            cvideo width="420" height="315" controls>
                <source src="assets/dog.mp4"/>
            </video>


YOUTUBE IFRAME

The <textarea> tag defines a multi-line text input control.

EXAMPLE

            <iframe width="420" height="315"
                src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/AAmsmTXTznM">
            </iframe>

Note: You must replace "/watch?=" part with embed/
