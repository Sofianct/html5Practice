HTML5 HEADINGS

HTML headings are defined with the <h1> to <h6> tags. <h1> defines the most important heading. <h6> defines the least important heading.
<h1>Heading 1</h1>

Heading 1
<h1>Heading 2</h1>

Heading 2
<h1>Heading 3</h1>

Heading 3
<h1>Heading 4</h1>

Heading 4
<h1>Heading 5</h1>
Heading 5
<h1>Heading 6</h1>
Heading 6
